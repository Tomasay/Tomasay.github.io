Sculpt January 2020

During the month of January 2020, I participated in a challenge called Sculpt January. A sculpting theme and prompt was given every day with the goal of creating very quick, prototype sculpts. Here are the themes I participated in along with the sculpts I created. I spent roughly 1-3 hours on each sculpt including designing, sculpting, shading, rendering, and compositing

All of the sculpts were created and textured in Blender, rendered using Eevee, and composited in Gimp

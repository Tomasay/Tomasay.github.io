Luminar

Luminar is a mixed reality web browser with realtime lighting and reflections for enhanced viewing experiences. Users can browse the web, stream media, and cast from their desktop

Check out the store page here


Luminar started off as a lighting prototype to try and get Unity lights to work in passthrough utilizing a custom shader:


With the addition of reflections, I created a demo for a mixed reality video player:


The prototype was well received on social media, so I decided to turn it into a fully functional web browsing app. I integrated the browser using Vuplex, and implemented some other settings and functionality such as a backlight, hand tracking, desktop streaming and more.

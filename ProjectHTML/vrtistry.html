Overview

VRtistry is an asymmetric VR party painting game where one player brings wild ideas to life in VR and friends compete to guess and outwit each other.

Think Pictionary in VR. Mobile players are given a fun prompt such as "What superhero does the vr player remind you of?". One of their answers is then chosen for the VR player to recreate. Instead of drawing on a 2d surface, the VR player gets to pose a mannequin that mimics their movement, and can then draw on or around the mannequin in space. The artwork is then showed to the mobile players to guess what it is for points.

VRtistry is planned to release on the Meta Quest store mid 2026. It is one of many party games in a larger project I am also developing: Party Crashers

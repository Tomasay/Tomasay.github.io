Overview

One of the projects I've worked on at Meta is Horizon Central: the hub world players land in when they open Horizon Worlds. Working alongside artists and designers, I implemented 3D assets, VFX, and runtime logic for the space.

Load Time Optimization

Horizon Central is built as a set of connected 50-player worlds, so players cross between world instances constantly and every crossing is a load. On Quest hardware, that transition is the difference between a hub that feels like one continuous place and one that feels like a menu.

Working with tech art team, I helped implement CDN-based optimizations and custom model loading particle effects and animations that brought load times between worlds to under 10 seconds while holding the hub's visual fidelity.
